Jide Kosoko education

The actor completed his primary and secondary school education in Lagos Island in Nigeria. He obtained his First School Leaving Certificate and West African Senior School Certificate from schools in Lagos.

Later, he joined Yaba College of Technology, commonly known as Yabatech, in Yanba, Lagos State Nigeria. He graduated with a Higher National Diploma (HND) in Business Administration.

Career

Jide began his career as a child actor. He starred in a 1964 TV production known as Makanjuola, playing the role of Alabi. He later joined the Awada Kerikeri group to perform with other actors and actresses such as Sunday Omobolanle, Lanre Hassan and Oga Bello.

He appeared as a guest on the TV show known as New Masquerades. In 1972, he formed his theatre troupe. Apart from acting, Jide has also produced his movies and stage plays.
