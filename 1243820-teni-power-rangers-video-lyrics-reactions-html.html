Teni - Power Rangers: video, lyrics, reactions

 Posted on June 10, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  890 words  |  Chauncey Koziol

As Teniola Apata's fans, we have a lot to be grateful for when it comes to the artist's delivery of instant hits. This year, she blessed us with Sugar Mummy which rocked our worlds. Now, the singer has just released yet another song, Teni - Power Rangers, which can only be described as extraordinary!

Teni - Power Rangers description

  • Release date: 14th June, 2019
  • Format: Audio, video
  • Genre: Afrobeat
  • Length: 3 minutes 35 seconds
  • Label: Dr Dolor Entertainment
  • Producer: Jaysynths Beatz
  • Video director: Toka McBaror

If you thought that Teni outdid herself in her previous hits, then you will be pleasantly surprised once you watch the video to Teni - Power Rangers. Devoid from nudity and all the rather icky performances that pass for entertainment in the 21st century, the song is one that all audiences will surely love, regardless of age, gender, or even genre preference.

Have you ever seen Teni in a dress? Wait, let me rephrase my question. Have you ever seen Teni in a ball gown together with a durag on her head? Well, no one would expect the magnificent artist to leave her durag behind, even when she decides to film a video in a majestic black ball gown. Watch the video to the song, and see how the star surprisingly enough pulls off this look. Only Teni can do this!
