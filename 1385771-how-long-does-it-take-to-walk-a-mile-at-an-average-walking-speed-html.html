The average time of walking this distance is 15 to 22 minutes, as some studies have determined. However, it is not the same for everyone, and is usually individual for every person, with multiple factors playing a key part in it.

The speed of your steps often depends on physical shape, age, gender, and the type of terrain. For instance, as a person ages, the speed always decreases.

It is much easier to walk on asphalt and concrete than, for example, on slippery and muddy grass terrain. Additionally, scientists have found out that men, on average, walk faster than women.

There are athletes who can conquer this distance rather fast and impress everyone with their brilliant timing.

The world record for the fastest one mile walk ever belongs to Tom Bosworth, who is a British Olympic race walker. This man holds three different world records, which is highly impressive.

In 2017, Tom Bosworth managed to complete a 1 mile walk in 5 minutes, 31 seconds and 08 milliseconds. His other notable achievements are breaking world records in the 3000m indoor race walk and the 3000m outdoor race walk, both in 2018.

What are different walking paces?

A person can walk at different speeds, depending on the purpose of the stroll and personal preferences. Typically, there are fast, moderate, and easy paces.

  • Fast: 7 min per kilometer or 11 min per mile
  • Moderate: 10 min per kilometer or 15 min per mile
  • Easy: 12.5+ min per kilometer or 20+ min per mile

Is walking a mile in 20 minutes good?

This is the average time of walking this distance for most people. So, if it took you 20 minutes to conquer it at a normal speed, it is completely good and healthy.

Training for different distances

Whether you are a marathon runner or a simple beginner who wants to stay in shape, it would be beneficial for you to gradually build up your distance. If you do this every day, your muscles and stamina will toughen up, and you will avoid getting blisters.

You should start with a distance that is comfortable with you and then add more every week. With time, building up your distance will help you achieve your goals, whether it is staying fit or training for a marathon.

Brisk pace: what is it?

Many experts recommend walking at a brisk pace, which means a little faster than you normally do. Usually, it is considered to be 100 steps per minute, which also equals 3 or 3.5 mph.

Walking at this speed helps improve your fitness, and gives you a lot of health benefits, including the improvement of your breathing, heart rate, coordination, and balance. It also helps to reduce the risk of developing chronic illnesses such as diabetes, cancer, or heart disease.

However, it is important to control your speed and distance so as not to overwhelm yourself. Like every athlete, you should start with small steps, and then gradually increase your distance and pace when you feel comfortable doing so.

Always remember to talk to your healthcare provider before developing a fitness program for yourself. Do not attempt anything drastic if you have pre-existing medical conditions, and listen to your body to prevent any unfortunate situations.
