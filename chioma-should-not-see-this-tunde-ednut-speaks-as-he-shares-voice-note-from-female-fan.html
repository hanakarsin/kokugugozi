Chioma Should Not See This Tunde Ednut Speaks As He Shares Voice Note From Female Fan

 Posted on March 28, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  214 words  |  Chauncey Koziol

Popular blogger and entertainer, Tunde Ednut has shared an audio message from a female fan of Davido.

In the voice note, the lady can be heard saying that she loves the singer’s smile.

Ednut, some days back shared a clip of the moment social media influencer, Ola of Lagos bumped into Davido and had to make a video of him.

Davido who could be seen in his car with his lawyer, Bobo J. Ajudua smiled at the camera as Ola of Lagos sang his praises.

Reacting to the video, a lady took to Ednut’s DM to drop a message praising Davido.

In the voice note, the lady could be heard stating that she so much likes the video adding that David’s smile makes her feel like getting pregnant.

However, captioning the video, Tunde told the lady to stop admiring and drooling over the crooner as he is in a serious relationship with his third baby mama, Chioma Rowland.

He advised the love-struck fan to look the other way because the father of four is already taken.

The blogger also hoped on a lighter note that Chioma would not come across the audio.

He wrote, “Please Oo! Chioma already has Davido Oo! No let chioma see this post Oo! These fans en! Na wa…”
