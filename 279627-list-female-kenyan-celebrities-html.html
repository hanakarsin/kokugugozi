Kenyan celebrities, especially female public figures, get plenty of attention from the public whenever they are spotted going about their usual activities. Some are known for their music and lifestyle, while others are famous because of their controversial nature. The Kenyan entertainment scene seldom lacks a trending celebrity for one reason or another.

It is sometimes challenging to keep up with Kenyan celebrity news because there is always something new and trending each day. Kenyan celebrities have to develop thick skin because they never know when the public will praise or criticise them. Some female public figures in the country have mastered how to handle fame, while others are still learning how to strike a balance between their private and public lives.

10. Kambua Manundu

Kambua Manundu is a Kenyan celeb whose faith in God inspires many Kenyans. She is known as a gospel singer, social media celebrity, and media personality. She hosted part of Citizen TV's Gospel Sunday show, Rauka, for 12 years before leaving the job in January 2022. Her songs include Nishikilie, Shukrani, Neema, Umetenda Mema, and Anatimiza.

The singer has a following of over 1.3 million on Instagram. She uses the platform to promote various brands, including Risiih Investments, Garnier Kenya, Phillips Kenya, and Ckei Homestyle & Interiors.

Kambua is married to Pastor Mathu, with whom she shares a son named Nathaniel, alias Nate. The couple was married for seven years without a child, and she was heavily trolled for not having children.

9. Lillian Muli

Lillian Muli is a celebrated news anchor and journalist who has been in the industry for over 15 years. Many people love her confidence, smile, and fluent language when presenting the news. Her career has earned her a significant fanbase, and she has over 1.4 million followers on Instagram.

The journalist went to Loreto Convent, Msongari, and later on, Daystar University. She also holds a Masters degree in Strategic Management from the University of Nairobi. She started her career in 2005 at KTN's creation division.

8. Wahu Kagwi

Rosemary Wahu Kagwi is a household name in the Kenyan entertainment industry. She started her singing career over two decades ago and remains relevant to date.

The tracks include Te Amo, Back It Up, and Feeling. Fans were elated to see the couple working together on music after many years of working on solo music projects.

5. Vera Sidika

4. Size 8 Reborn

Linet Munyali, alias, Size 8 Reborn, is a gospel artist, songwriter, social media personality, and ordained preacher. She has over 2.5 million followers on Instagram. The singer came into the limelight after Kenyan producer Clemo spotted her talent in music.

The Hillcrest School alumna started her music career as a secular artist. Shamba Boy, Vidonge, and Moto were some of her hit songs before getting saved. Later on, she became a born-again Christian and married Samuel Muraya, alias DJ Mo, with whom she has two kids. Unfortunately, the couple lost their unborn child in October 2021.

She now releases gospel songs such as Mateke, Afadhali Yesu, Pale Pale, and Yuko na Wewe. In December 2018, the singer graduated from Harvesters Global Church Bible Training School. In December 2021, she was ordained as a preacher in JCC Thika, under the mentorship of Pastor Kelvin Ephraim and Pastor Jackie Kelvin.s

3. Betty Kyalo

Who is the most famous celebrity in Kenya? Betty Mutei Kyalo is among the most famous female celebrities in contemporary Kenya. She has over 2.9 million followers on Instagram.

Betty is a media and television personality, social media personality, and businesswoman. She first came into the limelight several years back as a news anchor on the Mediamax Network Limited owned TV station, K24. She has also worked on Standard Group's TV station, KTN.

Betty's life has not been devoid of controversies. She was previously married to fellow media personality Dennis Okari, with whom she has a daughter named Ivanna. After the marriage ended, she was romantically linked to politician and Mombasa governor Hassan Joho.

Recently, she trended after her relationship with lawyer Nick Ndeda went sour. Despite the controversies, the media personality always shines. She runs a successful salon called Flair by Betty, a barbershop called Aftershave by Flair, and a clothing line known as BK Closet.

2. Akothee

Who is the richest celeb in Kenya? Esther Akoth Kokeyo, alias Akothee, is arguably the richest female celeb in Kenya. She has an estimated net worth of $10 million. Akothee is a musician, social media personality and influencer, and businesswoman.

The mother of five is almost always trending because she is a controversial figure. She has crowned herself the "president of single mothers" and is known for living life without regard for naysayers and critics.

Akothee became a teen mom after eloping with her ex-husband at 14. She has three adult daughters and two younger boys. She runs a tour and travel company called Akothee Safaris and is a realtor at Aknotela and Akothee Homes.

Additionally, she is a musician whose songs include Sweet Love featuring Diamond Platnumz, Give It To Me featuring Flavour, Yuko Moyoni, Djele Djele, and Nakupa Moyo featuring Bahati. She has over 3.1 million followers on Instagram and often uses the platform to promote brands such as Peptang, Rosy tissue, KPipes and fittings centre, and Sunrice.

1. Lupita Nyong'o

At the 2014 Oscar Awards, the world watched Lupita Amondi Nyong'o, a Kenyan actress born in Mexico City, Mexico, giving an inspiring speech. This was after she won the Best Performance by an Actress in a Supporting Role Oscar Award for her role in 12 Years a Slave.

Today, Lupita is a household name in Hollywood. The actress was born on 1st March 1983 to Dorothy Ogada Buyu and the current governor of Kisumu City. She was brought up in Kenya and went to Rusinga school.

When she was 16, her parents sent her back to Mexico to study Spanish before proceeding to Hampshire College, Massachusetts, for film studies. Who is most followed on Instagram in Kenya? Among female Kenyan celebrities, Lupita is the most followed, with over 9 million followers.

Besides 12 Years a Slave, the actress has also starred in Black Panther, Shuga, Star Wars: Episode VII - The Force Awakens, Queen of Katwe, Star Wars: Forces of Destiny, The 355, and Supa Sema.
