Who Is Paul Juda Dating? She's a Fellow Gymnast

U.S. Olympic Gold Medalist Paul Juda Is Dating a Fellow Award-Winning Gymnast From the University of Michigan

"I owe it all to you. No one pushes me like you. You are my everything," Paul said of his girlfriend.

By Elissa Noblitt

Updated July 31 2024, 1:47 p.m. ET

The Men's Gymnastics team from the United States is making waves this summer at the 2024 Paris Olympics. After the team earned their bronze medal and won the hearts of fans the world over, many want to know more about the team members and their personal lives. And aside from Stephen Nedoroscik, who has been dubbed the Clark Kent of the Olympic Games, one more male gymnast has captured the public's attention: Paul Juda.

Paul Juda, a first-generation American born to Polish immigrant parents, has inspired audiences with his determination, grit, and sheer talent — and no one is prouder of him than his family and his longtime girlfriend, who have been there for him every step of the way.

Who is Paul Juda dating?

Paul Juda is dating a fellow gymnast from Michigan.

When viewing Paul Juda's personal Instagram page, one doesn't have to scroll far to find out who he's dating. In fact, he often makes loving and supportive posts about his girlfriend, Reyna Guggino, who is a fellow gymnast from the University of Michigan.

In fact, a post made on February 4 and pinned on his profile congratulates Reyna for reaching an athletic goal of hers: "Just a few days ago we were watching an NCAA gymnastics meet and I told her that watching her break a 10 would be the greatest thing in the world. Unbeknownst to me, she would break that 10 just a few days later."

He continued, "I owe it all to you. No one pushes me like you. You are my everything. I love you."

Reyna is a gymnast for the Women's Gymnastics team at the University of Michigan, where she studies biology. She's from Tampa, Fla. and attended H.B. Plant High School where she was also a gymnast. She often posts snaps having fun with friends, as well as action shots of her gymnastics, cute pics with Paul, and more.

Reyna and Paul, who have been together since 2020, are an undeniably adorable couple. Photos shared on their social media pages feature them attending football games together and supporting each other's athletic careers.

In a few sweet snaps on her page, Reyna has called Paul the "guy who brings the most joy into [her] life" and her "home away from home."

After competing in the qualifying round of the Men's Gymnastics competition at the 2024 Summer Olympics, Paul gave a tearful shout out to Reyna and his family, who traveled to Paris to cheer him on.

"When we walked into the arena, I didn’t see anybody ... "[Eventually] I hear Reyna’s signature yell, and I was like, 'It’s coming from around that area,'" he said. "When I finally locked eyes with them and saw them ... I mean, I got a little emotional right after the pommel horse, because I was like, ‘They’re all here. They all flew hundreds and thousands of miles to come see me.'"
