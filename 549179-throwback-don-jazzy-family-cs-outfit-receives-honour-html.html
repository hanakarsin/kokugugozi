Throwback: Don Jazzy And Family In C&amp;S Outfit As He Receives Honour

 Posted on March 27, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  210 words  |  Chauncey Koziol

Who would have thought Mavin Records boss, Don Jazzy emerged from Eternal Sacred Order of the Cherubim and Seraphim (C&S) church?

Well his father, Collins Enebeli shared the old family photos of himself with Don Jazzy, D’Prince and other children on his Facebook page recently.

As part of the church’s celebration of 90th anniversary in Nigeria, the beat maker was honoured as one of the worthy offspring of the church during a recognition service at the church’s headquarters in Tejuosho, Lagos.
