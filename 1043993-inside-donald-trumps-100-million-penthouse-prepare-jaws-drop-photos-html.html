However, we doubt it will be easy for him to say goodbye to his luxurious three-level penthouse, crowning  the 66th floor of Trump Tower.

It boasts breathtaking views of Central Park and over-the-top decor, where gold, diamonds, lavish wood and ivory furniture, ceilings painted with scenes from the classical Greek myths, risqué statues and many more.

The penthouse is currently home to Trump, his third wife Melania, 45, and their son Barron, 9. Trump also has four elder children, Donald Jr., Ivanka, Eric and Tiffany.

Trump Tower which was completed in 1983 is home to not only the billionaire businessman but also to retail stores and other businesses.

It was used as a platform for his presidential campaign. Moreover, celebrities like actor Bruce Willis and soccer player Cristiano Ronaldo also lived there.

But the Louis XI-style apartment is just one of Donald Trump's luxurious properties. His other grandiose mansion named  Mar-A-Lago sits on 80,000 square feet in ritzy Palm Beach, Florida.

Trump purchased the estate for $5 million in 1985 and had the entire thing renovated with 58 bedrooms, 33 bathrooms, a 29-foot marble top dining table and 12 fireplaces.

He and his family often spend weekends and vacation at the luxurious residence. The property has perfectly landscaped gardens, pools and views of the Caribbean blue ocean.
