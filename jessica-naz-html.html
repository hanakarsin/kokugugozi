Jessica Naz - Age, Family, Bio

 Posted on March 25, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  93 words  |  Hettie Henneman

Professional model from Miami who was Maxim France's May/June Cover Girl in 2018. She has 600,000 followers on Instagram.

Before Fame

Her first Instagram post was on March 9, 2013, and was a photo of a bunny wearing a Brazil cap. The caption was a bunny and love heart eyes emoji. 

Trivia

She is represented by DAS Management, Wilhelmina Models Miami, Revolution Management, THE FACE models and Rebels MGMT. 

Family Life

She was born in Miami on the 19th of July, 1998. 

Associated With

Chase Carter, Lais Ribeiro and Johana Bahamón have all been 2018 Maxim Cover Girls.
