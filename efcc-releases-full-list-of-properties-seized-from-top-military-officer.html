EFCC Releases Full List Of Properties Seized From Top Military Officer

 Posted on June 30, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  474 words  |  Lourie Helzer

The Economic and Financial Crimes Commission (EFCC) has released the full list of 24 properties seized from an unnamed top military officer.

In a statement released on Tuesday, the anti-graft agency said it has secured the final forfeiture of the properties worth N11 billion to the Nigerian Government.

According to the EFCC, Justice N.E. Maha of the Federal High Court in Abuja granted the order for the final forfeiture of the properties on Monday.

The agency stated that the forfeiture follows an application filed by the EFCC in May 2020, after the court granted an order for interim forfeiture of the properties.

It added that following the application for final forfeiture, the court ordered that notices be published in national newspapers “inviting persons with interest in the properties to show cause why they should not be finally forfeited to the federal government”.

The EFCC said that with no such interest shown by any entity, Justice Maha ordered the final forfeiture of the properties to the Federal Government of Nigeria.
