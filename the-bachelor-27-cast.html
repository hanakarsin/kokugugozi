Here Are All the Details You Need About 'The Bachelor' Season 27 Cast

By Tatayana Yomary

Jan. 5 2023, Updated 3:30 p.m. ET

Buckle your seatbelts, Bachelor Nation! Season 27 of The Bachelor will soon be upon us. After fans watched The Bachelorette Season 19 finale, the world learned that Zach Shallcross, one of Rachel Recchia’s many suitors, won the coveted title as the new bachelor.

As The Bachelorette Season 19 continued, fans were able to see Zach recall learning that he would be the next Bachelor. Additionally, fellow leads were able to give Zach some advice on what to expect and how to carry himself during his journey. Plus, Zach got the opportunity to meet a few women that were going to compete for his heart.

Now that the forthcoming Season 27 of The Bachelor slated to hit the small screen in late-January 2023, Bachelor Nation is excited to meet all the women. So of course, we've got you covered.

Here’s everything that we know.

Here are the 30 women vying for Zach Shallcross's heart.

Zach Shallcross will be The Bachelor’s next lead. And while the news has brought upon mixed reviews from Bachelor Nation, everyone is excited to see the women prepared to snag the man.

Keep scrolling to get acquainted with the beautiful bunch.

1. Aly

First up, we have the gorgeous Aly that hails from Atlanta, Ga. The 26-year-old beauty currently works as a healthcare strategist and is said to be "a hopeless romantic" who has "even ended serious relationships because her exes weren't ready for the real deal," per her ABC bio.

2. Anastasia

Next, we have 30-year-old Anastasia. The San Diego resident is ready for love, and "when it comes to finding a husband, Anastasia is looking for a mature man who has his life together and knows what he wants," per her ABC bio.

3. Ariel

It's time to get acquainted with Ms. Ariel. The 28-year-old is a marketing executive from New York City that "comes from a big, loving Ukrainian family and is looking to find love like her parents have," per her ABC bio.

4. Bailey

Folks that have been keeping up with the latest development in Bachelor Nation are probably familiar with 27-year-old Bailey. We first met the Nashville native at The Bachelorette Season 19, Part 2 finale.

In the finale, Bailey gave Zach a mantra to remember her name, "On the daily, I'm going to be thinking of Bailey." It's pretty cheesy, but when it comes to finding love on The Bachelor, you have to give it all you got.

5. Becca

Hello Becca! The nursing student from Burbank, Calif., has a deep affinity for romance novels and has love and love only on her mind. The 25-year-old Mexican bombshell says that "she's looking for someone she has a natural chemistry with who isn't afraid to be goofy sometimes," per her ABC bio.

6. Brianna

Next, we have Brianna — who we also met at The Bachelorette Season 19 finale — who hails from Jersey City, N.J. The entrepreneur has her own beauty company and is ready to find her "ride-or-die partner with whom she can travel to new places," per her ABC bio. Plus, Brianna is not a fan of Netflix and chill. We love a queen with standards!

7. Brooklyn

Another beauty we had the privilege of meeting at The Bachelorette Season 19 finale is 25-year-old Brooklyn, a rodeo racer from Stillwater, Okla. While speaking with Zach, she shared that she wanted him to be the reason why she decides to move back to her native Texas, which is where Zach lives.

8. Cara

It's time to meet Cara! The 27-year-old corporate recruiter currently resides in Pittsburgh, Penn. Like many of us, "Cara has tried dating apps and being set up, but she is way more excited about this new, unique opportunity to find love with Zach," per her ABC bio. Hopefully, she gets the happy ending she deserves!

9. Catherine "Cat"

Bachelor Nation also had the pleasure of meeting Cat on The Bachelorette Season 19 finale. The 26-year-old dancer and beauty queen is from New York City, and she's ready for love. In her ABC bio, Cat shares that ready to find someone who accepts her for who she is and will make her a priority.

Maybe she'll be the first contestant to dance her way to a final rose. Only time will tell!

10. Charity

Next, we have beautiful 26-year-old Charity. Hailing from Columbus, Ga., Charity has southern charm that crosses over into her work as a child and family therapist. After giving herself to others, Charity is now ready for a future husband.

"Charity hopes her future husband is honest, loyal and shares similar morals. She says she is really excited to meet Zach and thinks the two of them could be a perfect match on paper, Charity says in her ABC bio.

11. Christina M.

Christina M. is another familiar face from The Bachelorette Season 19 finale. The 26-year-old resides in Nashville and works as a content creator. Upon meeting Zach, she allowed her bubbly personality to shine and presented Zach with a shot off whiskey and said that it "tastes like home."

12. Davia

Next up, we have 25-year-old Davia who works as a marketing manager. Davia currently resides in Charleston and is not shy about yearning for marriage. In her ABC bio, Davia reveals that she's been through a to in life but "she's looking for an honest man who will prioritize their relationship above all else."

13. Gabi

Hello Gabi! The 25-year-old currently resides in Pittsford, Vt. and works as an account executive. In Gabi's ABC bio, the beauty got candid about coming from a loving family. So naturally, Gabi is ready for marriage and is using her "parents's marriage as the blueprint for the kind of love she hopes to find."

14. Genevie

Shout out to the DMV! Genevie, a 26-year-old neonatal nurse that resides in Baltimore City, Md., is looking for Mr. Right! In her ABC bio, Genvie shares that she's a diehard Baltimore Ravens fan and is currently looking for a loyal and adventurous man and "only dates to find the real deal."

15. Greer

At 24 years old, Greer is one of the youngest of the bunch, but she's ready to put her hat into the ring for Zach's heart. The Houston native works as a medical sales representative and enjoys shopping at flea markets, per her ABC bio.

16. Holland

Another 24-year-old in the mix is the gorgeous Holland. The beauty works as an insurance marketer in Boca Raton, Fla., and she has a deep affinity for wine and sushi. In Holland's ABC bio, she expressed that "the top things [she] is looking for in a relationship are unwavering commitment, loyalty, and a genuine personality."

17. Jessica "Jess"

Jess, who is the youngest of all the contestants, is said to have a heart of gold and "a sweet soul." According to Jess's ABC bio, she works as an e-commerce coordinator and "describes herself as a hopeless romantic and is ready for her fairytale romance worthy of a Taylor Swift love song." Interesting!

18. Kaitlyn "Kaity"

Next, we have Kaity! Judging by Kaity's ABC bio, the 27-year-old appears to check all the boxes for a potential perfect match for Zach. The beauty works as an ER nurse in Austin, is said to have a "great sense of humor, and is extremely down to earth." As for love, Kaity is all in and believes that she "has so much love to give to the right man."

19. Katherine

It looks like we have another nurse in the bunch! Katherine, who resides in Tampa, Fla. works as an RN. And while Katherine is passionate about her work, she shares that "her No. 1 priority is to find her perfect match," per her ABC bio. So, will the witty knockout win Zach's heart? Only time will tell.

20. Kimberly

Hello Kimberly! The 30-year-old is a Puerto Rican beauty with her eye on the prize — Zach's heart. In her ABC bio, Kimberly shares that she was in a six-year relationship that ended due to her partner not being ready for the next steps. Now, "Kimberly is ready to find her husband and start a family of her own." Get ready, Zach!

21. Kylee

Kylee, a 25-year-old from Charlotte, also joins the group as an NBA dancer-turned-postpartum nurse. The beauty explains in her bio that while she may be young, she's ready to settle down and "live the soccer mom life."

22. Chandralekha "Lekha"

Here we have Lehka! The Indian beauty is 29-years-old and currently works as a financial advisor in Miami. Lehka shares in her bio that her parents entered an arranged marriage, but they are very supportive in her hopes to "find the one" the traditional way — well, sort of.

23. Madison

Next, we have Madison! The 27-year-old is a business owner from Fargo, N.D. According to her bio, Madison became a caretaker for her younger sisters, which speaks to her caring spirit. That said, she's excited to continues the rest of her life and is hopeful and "more than ready to find someone to share it with."

24. Mercedes

Hello beautiful! Mercedes, a 24-year-old nonprofit case manager from Bloomfield, Iowa, is prepared to put her best food forward to snag Zach's heart. Per Mercedes's bio, she enjoys night outs on the town, but she also loves staying in.

25. Olivia L.

Olivia L. is from Rochester, New York. For work, Olivia makes a living as a patient care technician. Her bio shares that she has a deep affinity for basketball, which she considered to be her first love. However, Olivia is now focused on finding a fulfilling relationship.

26. Olivia M.

It's time to welcome a second Olivia to the bunch, Olivia M. The 25-year-old is a stylist from Cincinnati — she's a small town girl with big dreams of finding the greatest love of her life. In her bio, Olivia shares that she enjoys hot yoga. binge-watching TV shows, and enjoys a great read.

27. Sonia

Our next lovely lady goes by the name Sonia. She's 29 years old, works as a project manager in Long Island, N.Y. and is finally ready to meet her match after a string of failed serious relationships.

28. Vanessa

Next up, we have the lovely Vanessa! The 23-year-old restaurant marketer from Baton Rouge, La. The bright-smiled beauty is a foodie that's very passionate about her work, but she's also deeply passionate about love. Let's see if she can woo Zach!

29. Victoria J.

Next up, we have Victoria J. The makeup artist from Fort Worth, Texas knows her way around all things beauty but needs a bit of help in the romance department. In Victoria's bio, she shares that her parents have been married for over 40 years and hopes to find the same love in a future partner.

Viktoria E.

Last but certainly not least, we have Viktoria E! The Austrian beauty works as a nanny, which speaks to her love and admiration of children. Viktoria has since relocated to New York City and has high hopes of finding her dream man.

When will Season 27 of 'The Bachelor' premiere?

Alexa, play Jay-Z “On To The Next One.” Gabby and Rachel’s season is old news, and ABC has officially announced that Season 27 of The Bachelor will be hitting the small screen in late January 2023.

Gather yourselves, folks! The drama will begin very soon.

Season 27 of The Bachelor premieres Monday, Jan. 23, 2023 at 8 p.m. EST on ABC.
