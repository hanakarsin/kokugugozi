Omoni Oboli biography

Omoni Oboli was born on 22nd April 1978 in Benin City, Edo State, Nigeria. She is the daughter of the late Chief Mattew Eriyovwe Ukey, a well-known retired permanent secretary of the Delta Civil Service Commission.

Not much is known about Omoni Oboli family, however the Nollywood star grew up along with one sibling, a younger sister whose details are not known.

How old is Omoni Oboli?

As of 2021, Omoni Oboli age is 43 years old. The actress celebrates her birthday on 22nd April every year.

Education

Omoni Oboli attended primary and secondary education at the Delta Steel Company primary school and the Delta Steel Company Technical high school.

She, later on, joined the University of Benin and graduated with a degree in foreign languages, majoring in French. Omoni also has a certificate in digital film making from the New York Film Academy.

Career

Upon graduating from college, Omoni decided to pursue acting, something that she always had dreamt of doing when she grows up.

She made her acting debut in 1996 when she appeared in the film Bitter Encounter. Her acting skills paved the way for her in the film industry. So she began playing lead roles in various movies.

After acting for a while, the actress took a break to further her studies. She returned to the Nigerian film industry as part of the cast of the 2009 film, Entanglement.

That same year, she also played a major role in Kunle Afolayan's award-winning film, The Figurine.

From 2009 to date, she has produced and co-produced many movies. Her top productions include Wives on Strike, The Rivals and many more.
