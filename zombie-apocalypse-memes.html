21 Zombie Memes To Prepare You For The Inevitable Undead Apocalypse

By Mustafa Gatollari

Nov. 13 2018, Updated 12:27 p.m. ET

Zombies are sort of the hot thing right now. We're all trying to prep for the eventual scenario of being in our own zombie filled world, fighting for survival. What will we do? Where will we go? All questions we've all considered at one point in our lives.

Although I know that living is a post-apocalyptic world filled with zombies would be a dang nightmare and ultimately tragic for me and my family, I still can't help but wonder about how awesome it would be to live in a world filled with slow moving monsters I could run over with a truck and just go to town on with a sword. Maybe even just climb somewhere really high and wait it out, though that could have its downsides.

Trouble is, if a zombie outbreak were to occur, I'd most likely be one of the first people turned into the undead, I mean statistically speaking, there's no way I'd survive. The odds are just stacked against you.

So to help comfort me while I ponder this sad fact of life, I decided to lighten up the mood by checking out some hilarious zombie memes. Because if I'm going to become a member of an undead horde in search of humans to munch on, I'd want to be able to laugh about it.
