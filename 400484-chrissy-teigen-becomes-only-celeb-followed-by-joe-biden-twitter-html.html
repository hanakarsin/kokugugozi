Chrissy Teigen is now the only celebrity Twitter account being followed by the official US presidential account, @POTUS.

The model and businesswoman had previously been blocked by the account while Trump was in office and remained one of his most most outspoken critics, especially on Twitter.

With the Biden administration having been sworn in already, Chrissy took the chance to jokingly request to be followed by the new president after having been blocked for four years.
